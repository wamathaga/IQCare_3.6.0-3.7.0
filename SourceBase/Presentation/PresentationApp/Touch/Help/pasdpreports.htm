Facility Statistics Report

 

Unlike the Patient Reports, the Facility Statistics Report is accessed through the IQCare-PADMT Application's  Home page

 

1.To access a Facility Statistics report, select a site in the Facility drop down in the Home page.  The Total Number of Patients and Active Patients will be automatically displayed in the Statistics section.

2.Click on the required Statistic report link to view (Lost To Follow Up List or Due For Care Ended List)

 

•To print the Report, click the Print button at the top right corner of the report; your native printer dialog will display. All pages within the form will be printed
