Clinical Forms

There are 7 clinical forms available for IQCare-PADMT.

•Registration - patient demographics, prior ART treatment history

•Visit - current physical status, presenting complaints, adverse effects

•Pharmacy - pharmacy order and dispensing for a wide variety of drugs, including ARVs and antibiotics

•Laboratory - laboratory order and results entry for a wide variety of lab tests

•Non-Visit Clinical Note - free text note area for patient information collected outside of a regular visit

•Immunisations - listing of immunisations from the Road to Health booklet

•Care Ended - inactivating a patient due to transfer out, lost to follow up or death

 

 

The registration and immunisation form are "one-time" forms; meaning that there is only one instance of the form. If you click on the registration or immunisation button in the patient home page or from View Existing Forms page, you will always see one individual form, filled with data that was entered in the past. You can update this data and re-save.

 

The visit, pharmacy, laboratory, non-visit clinical note and care ended forms are forms created based on a visit or form date. As time passes, there will be many instances of these forms which you can access through the View Existing Forms page.
