Data Reconstruction and Date Constraints

  

If your facility has decided to go through a data reconstruction exercise, then you will need to adjust the facility set up to allow for past dates. When data is collected at your facility as part of providing services to patients in an ongoing capacity, IQCare executes field level validation and does not allow you to enter erroneous dates. For example, the expected delivery date of a pregnant woman would never be in the past and so, IQCare will not let you enter a date in the past for this field in static data entry forms. However, when your facility is doing data reconstruction, all of the data is in the past. To remove the date constraints, you will need to go to the facility set up page and configure IQCare to accept past dates. When your facility has completed data reconstruction, you will return to the facility set up page and re-apply the date constraints.

 

 

Facility Setup- Turn off date constraint

1.Login to IQCare.

2.Navigate to Administration> Facility setup on Admin level navigation.

3.Select a facility to open in ‘Edit facility’ mode. Verify link to ‘Turn off Date Constraint’.

4.Click on Checkbox to remove date constraints from the application, click on update to save changes. Logout and login application.

5.The date constraints work on following fields and forms
