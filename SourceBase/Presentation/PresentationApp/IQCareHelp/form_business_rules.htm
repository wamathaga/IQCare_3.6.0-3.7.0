Form Business Rules

  

Form Business Rules

  

To set Form business rules, click on the 'Form Business Rule' button for a form.

 

 

Enter the business rules in the dialog that display.

 

 

Business Rule

Definition

Single Visit

The form is configured to be saved only once. Though this can be updated by admin.

Multiple Visit

New form can be saved on multiple visits.Saved form can be updated by admin.

Signature on each tab

The tabs on form can be saved by different users if the check box for 'signature on each tab' is selected.

Active if current age >= min __ and < max __ years

If the patient's age is greater than or equal to a numeric value (integer age) AND strictly less then a numeric value (integer or decimal maximum age) then enable the form.

Active if female

If the patient is female, then enable the form

Active if male

If the patient is male, then enable the form.
