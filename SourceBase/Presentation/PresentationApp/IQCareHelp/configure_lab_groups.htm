Configure Lab Groups

  

IQCare provides the ability to create lab groups/panels. This is a grouping of lab test that when selected in the laboratory form, rows for each test in the group is automatically displayed in the form and lab test results can be entered.

 

Configuring lab groups is a two-step process.

1.Create the lab group name in Additional Labs as a lab group

2.Go to the lab group custom list and find the newly entered group name. Open the record and add in the tests that make up the panel. Save.

 

Step 1: Create the lab group name in Additional Labs

1.Navigate to Administration>Customize list

 

2.Select Additional labs from list of Customize lists.

3.Click on ‘Add’ button in the page that opens

 

4.Add Laboratory test by entering Laboratory Test name, Department and select the Data Type as ‘Group’ .

5.Click save.

IQCare adds this new additional lab as a group.

 

 

Step 2: Configure the lab group

1.Navigate to Customize list> Lab Test Groups

 

 

2.A list of lab groups displays. Click on the lab group added. The lab selection multi-select list displays.

 

3.Using the check boxes, select all of the lab tests in this group.

4.Click save

 

 

Display of lab groups in the laboratory form

1.To check your work and display of the group in the laboratory form, select a patient in a service.

2.Create new form> Lab form and select the custom lab group just added. Note that the lab group name display but the lab tests do not yet display.

 

 

3.Navigate to view existing form and click on saved lab order
