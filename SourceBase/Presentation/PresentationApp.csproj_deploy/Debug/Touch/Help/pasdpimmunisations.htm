•This is a one-time form, meaning that there is one form for the patient and as immunisations are administered to the patient, they are documented in one form.

 

•You can access the immunisation form either from the patient home page or via view existing forms. The date the immunisation form is first saved is the date of the form.

 

•The immunisation form matches the South African Road to Health booklet. As well, if any other immunisations are given, they can be documented at the end of the form.

 

•To print the form, click the Print button at the bottom of the form; your native printer dialog will display. All pages within the form will be printed.

Add a new immunisation

1.Click on the immunisations button in the patient home page.

2.Find the time frame and immunisation that is being administered

3.Change the Administered status to "Yes" and enter the date administered.

4.If the immunisation is given to catch up for missed immunisations, then check the Catch Up Given Today check box.

5.When complete, click the save button in the top navigation of IQCare-PADMT

 

Add an immunisation not in the Road to Health booklet

1.Navigate and open the patient's immunisation form

2.Scroll down to the end of the immunisation form

3.Enter the name of the immunisation and the date given and click the add button to add the immunisation to the grid.

4.Repeat adding additional immunisations until complete

5.Click the save button in the top navigation of the IQCare-PADMT screen
