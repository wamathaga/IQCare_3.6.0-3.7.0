Visit

The visit form captures information on a per visit basis and is likely to change over time. The visit form covers the patient's presenting complaints, any adverse drug reactions and

 

1.Click on the Visit button to open the form.

2.There are two required fields in the form:

a.Clinical Status tab: Visit Date

b.Pharmacy & Lab tab; Substitutions/Interruptions select list

3.Enter the other data fields as available, noting that by clicking yes or no on some fields or by selecting an option in a select list, additional fields may display.

4.Click the tab label to open a tab.

5.When complete, click the save button at the top left of the screen.

 

•To print the form, click the Print button at the bottom of the form; your native printer dialog will display. All pages within the form will be printed.
