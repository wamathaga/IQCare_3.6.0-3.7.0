The pharmacy feature in IQCare-PADMT displays a listing of past and current orders in the Pharmacy Order grid screen and allows you to fill an existing order, refill an existing order or start a new order.

 

Basic pharmacy order flow:

1.In the Patient Home Page, click on the pharmacy button.

2.In the Pharmacy Order grid page, click on the New Order button to initiate a new order.

3.Fill in the pharmacy form section by section, entering the ordered by and ordered date and saving.

4.Return to the pharmacy order grid and click on the dispense link found in the Action column.

5.Open the drug dispensing grid section and enter the number of items dispensed along with dispensed by and dispensed date and save.

If there are no refills, then the order is complete. You can view the record but you cannot edit it.

 

The Pharmacy order grid

In the pharmacy order grid, you can see a listing of all of the the patient's pharmacy past, filled orders as well as any new orders not yet filled or orders that have refills associated with it.
