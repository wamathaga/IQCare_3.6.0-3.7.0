Registration

 

 

The registration form captures key patient demographic and caregiver information as well as the patient's HIV care and treatment history.

 

1.Click on the Registration button to open the form.

2.Enter data in the first tab,

a.Note that fields highlighted in yellow are required fields.

b.If you enter a birth date the age is automatically calculated; if you enter an age and click the Cal DOB button, the date of birth is calculated according to the number of years and months. If you enter only years, the date of birth will be set to June 15th or the calculated year.

3.Click the tab label to open a tab.

4.When complete, click the save button at the top left of the screen.

 

•To print the form, click the Print button at the bottom of the form; your native printer dialog will display. All pages within the form will be printed.
