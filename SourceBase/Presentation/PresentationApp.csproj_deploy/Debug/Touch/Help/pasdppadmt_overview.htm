IQCare-PADMT Overview

IQCare-PADMT (Paediatric ART Data Management Tool) is a touch screen electronic medical record used to capture paediatric HIV Care and Treatment data in a paperless mode. The motivation for this application is be able to capture longitudinal clinical data quickly and easily. The ability to call up an existing patient and see the snap shot of the patent's clinical status as well as run patient reports is expected to help the clinicians make better informed decisions.

 

The success of PADMT is directly dependent on the people who contributed to the content of the software, specifying features, workflow, business rules and data indicators. The dedication of these contributors and others not named here is greatly appreciated and acknowledged
