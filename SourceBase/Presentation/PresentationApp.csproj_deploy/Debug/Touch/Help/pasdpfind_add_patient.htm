Find/Add Patient

You can always get to the Find/Add patient feature from all PADMT screens.

 

1.When you log into into IQCare-PADMT, you will automatically be taken to the Find/Add screen where you can search for an existing patient or add a new patient.

 

2.When you are actively working in the application, you can always get to the Find/Add screen by clicking on the Find/Add button found on every page.
