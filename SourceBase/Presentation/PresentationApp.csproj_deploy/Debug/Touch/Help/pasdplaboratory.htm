Basic Laboratory order flow:

 

1.In the Patient Home Page, click on the Laboratory button.

2.In the Laboratory Order Summary grid page, click on the New Order button to initiate a new order.

3.Fill in the Laboratory form section by section, entering the ordered by and ordered date.

4.When complete, click the save button in the top navigation of IQCare-PADMT

 

•To print the form, click the Print button at the bottom of the form; your native printer dialog will display. All pages within the form will be printed.
