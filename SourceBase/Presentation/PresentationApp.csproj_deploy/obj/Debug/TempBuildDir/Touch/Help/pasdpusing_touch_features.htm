Entering field values and text

You can enter data using either a touch screen or a keyboard or both.

 

Touch Screen

The touch screen data entry displays an on-screen keyboard for text and numeric data. Using your finger, touch anywhere in the data field and then begin using the on-screen keyboard to select text or number characters. Use the tab option to move to the next field or touch on the next field with your finger.

 

Some fields that are numbers only, such as telephone numbers, a numeric on-screen keyboard will display.

 

Keyboard

The keyboard data entry is like any other application. Tab into or click your mouse in the field and text or numbers into the field; use your mouse to display select lists.

 

This is an example of a fully enable on-screen keyboard. This keyboard works like a regular physical keyboard; tapping action with your fingers selects the text characters.

 

This is an example of a numeric only on-screen keyboard. This kind of keyboard limits the user to enter numbers only.

 

Required fields

Required fields are indicated by being filled with a yellow color. Upon saving, if theses kind of fields are not filled in, in addition to a pop up alert saying that this field must be filled in before saving, the field color changes to orange.

 

Example of a required field.

 

Form up and down arrows / sweep up and down

To move up and down within a tab, either top on the arrow with your finger or click on the arrow with your mouse. You can use a sweeping motion with your finger to quickly move vertically through the page.

To move to different tabs, tap or click on the tab name.

 

 

Pop-up screens

IQCare-PADMT uses pop-up screens extensively. These pop-ups display on top of the main screen and the main screen becomes grayed out. Enter data as you would in any other form and when done, use the "X" to close. The data entered into a pop-up is "remembered" by the application and saved when you save the form.

 

 

Saving form data/Moving back to the patient home page

 

 

Processing in progress

After clicking a tile on the patient home page or clicking the save icon, IQCare-PADMT processes the screen elements and data. When this is taking place, a light blue shield covers the form so that you cannot enter data. The moving squares indicate that the computer is processing. When complete, IQCare-PADMT will display the fresh form or home page. Please wait until processing is complete to proceed.
