Patient Slider

The patient slider displays all of the patient's key demographic, pharmacy and lab information as well as patients alerts.

 

1. To access the Patient slider, click on the Patient name tag

 

 Patient Slider Display

•Patient demographic information

•Latest labs display – with up and down errors

•Latest regimen

•ART Therapy “report”

•Growth curve display with data overlay

•CD4 and Viral load graph

•Weight and BMI graph

•Visual alerts displayed in one box such as:

onext viral load lab date

oOut of drugs

oLabs ordered (but not yet entered)

oPharmacy ordered (but displease acknowledgment not yet entered)
